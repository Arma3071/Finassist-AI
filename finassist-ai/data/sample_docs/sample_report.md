# Q3 Financial Overview

## Revenue Performance

The company reported revenue of $482 million for Q3, an increase of 14%
year-over-year. Growth was driven primarily by strong demand in the
enterprise software segment, which contributed $290 million, and a
recovering hardware division, which contributed $192 million.

## Profitability

Gross margin improved to 61.2%, up from 58.7% in the prior year quarter.
Operating expenses were tightly managed, rising only 4% despite the
revenue growth, resulting in operating income of $96 million.

## Balance Sheet Highlights

Cash and cash equivalents stood at $1.1 billion at quarter end. Total
debt remained flat at $420 million. The current ratio improved to 2.3,
reflecting a strengthening liquidity position.

## Outlook

Management reiterated full-year guidance of $1.9-2.0 billion in revenue,
citing continued momentum in enterprise contracts and a stabilizing
macroeconomic environment.
